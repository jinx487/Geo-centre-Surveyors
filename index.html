Geo-Centre History

Founded in 2017 in Kikuyu Kiambu county, by Simon Manyeki Wainaina, our surveying firm began with a vision to provide precise and reliable surveyng services.
In our early years we undertook our first projects which included subdivision, partition, transfer of plots and provided title deeds. Which inturn helped us grow and establish a reputation for excellence.
As technology evolved, so did we. We expanded our services to include GIS and RS services adopting state-of-the-art tools like Arc-GIS and Q-GIS, exanding our expertise to meet the diverse needs of our clients.
Today, we take pride in our dedicated team of professionals who continue to embody our core values, striving to provide exceptional services while fostering strong relationships within the communities we serve.

About

At Geo-centre Surveyors, we specialize in a comprehensive range of surveying services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our expertise spans various sectors, ensuring that we provide accurate, reliable, and innovative solutions. Here’s a closer look at our core activities:

Cadastral Surveys
Cadastral surveys are vital for establishing land boundaries and property ownership. Our experienced surveyors conduct meticulous cadastral surveys to define the precise limits of parcels of land. This service is essential for land transactions, property development, and dispute resolution. We ensure compliance with local regulations and provide clients with comprehensive documentation, including boundary maps and legal descriptions, to facilitate smooth land transfers and development processes.



Land/Title Surveys
Our land/title survey services focus on providing accurate measurements and descriptions of properties for legal purposes. We conduct thorough examinations of existing records and perform fieldwork to provide clients with detailed reports and maps. Our goal is to help clients navigate the complexities of land ownership and title issues with confidence.



Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Remote Sensing (RS) Services
Our GIS and RS services leverage advanced technology to capture, analyze, and visualize spatial data. We utilize satellite imagery, aerial surveys, and drone technology to gather precise geographic information. Our GIS solutions empower clients to make informed decisions by integrating data into user-friendly maps and models. Whether it’s for urban planning, environmental assessments, or infrastructure development, our GIS and RS services provide valuable insights that enhance project outcomes.

Projects

Subdivision

Our recent plot subdivision project involved transforming a large tract of land into smaller, well-defined plots, catering to both residential and commercial needs. The project, located in Kajiado kaputiei, was driven by the client's goal of optimizing land use while adhering to local zoning and environmental regulations. We began with an extensive topographical and boundary survey to map the terrain and accurately define the land’s borders. Using modern surveying tools such as GPS and total stations, and leveraging software like AutoCAD for precise design, we created a subdivision plan that ensured optimal plot sizes, road access, and utility planning.The final subdivision plan resulted in 118 plots, complete with access roads and infrastructure, approved by local authorities. This project will support future development, contributing to the region’s growth by offering new housing and commercial opportunities while ensuring sustainable land use. The successful execution of the subdivision is a testament to our team's technical expertise and commitment to delivering high-quality, compliant projects.
